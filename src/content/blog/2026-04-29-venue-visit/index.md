## Discussions & Key Decisions

### 1. Venue Walkthrough
The team met with the venue manager and conducted a full walkthrough of the venue grounds, available open-air structures, and storage warehouses (go-downs) that can be utilized. 
- The venue manager indicated that a CAD drawing of the entire site layout is available and will be shared.

### 2. Initial Layout Planning
The team discussed baseline parameters for space distribution. Designs will be adapted to fit:
- Existing permanent structures (which reduces staging build costs).
- Open space distribution to facilitate comfortable foot traffic.

### 3. Parking Assessment
Parking feasibility was determined to be excellent, with an estimated capacity of **2,000–3,000 cars**. Allocating specific parking zones and entry/exit points will be essential to ensure smooth vehicle flow.
