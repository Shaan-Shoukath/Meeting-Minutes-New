## Discussions & Key Decisions

### 1. Maker Faire Licensing (Make Community)
The team reviewed the outcome of last week's conference call with the Make team.
- **Licensing Fee:** The fee for the mini/unfeatured Maker Faire tier is **USD 2,500**.
- **Decision:** The committee agreed to proceed with the USD 2,500 tier, enabling full usage of the Make branding, graphics assets, and global promotion.
- **Licensing payment:** Installment/EMI options are available. The team will reach out to KSUM leadership (Tom, COO) and corporate partners (MD of Robu) to coordinate funding.

### 2. Draft Sponsorship Structure

- **Gold:** ~INR 8–10 lakhs
- **Silver (Community-focused):** ~INR 2–3 lakhs
- These values are initial guidelines and will be refined based on the pitch deck feedback.
