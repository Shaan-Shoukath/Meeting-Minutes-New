## Discussions & Key Decisions

### 1. Maker Faire Partnership & Licensing
The team discussed the licensing and coordination steps required to align the event with the official global Maker Faire brand.
- **Outreach call:** A meeting with Jennifer from Make's team is planned to clarify branding guidelines and participation criteria.
- David (Make community manager) shared official contact details to facilitate onboarding.
- **Branding release:** Domain (`makerfaire.in`) and public announcements will remain pending until official licensing is locked.
- The team studied website handling and local adaptations in Hyderabad and Shenzhen to guide the Kerala edition.

### 2. Community & Partner Pipeline Management
To establish transparency and prevent duplicate efforts, a shared system has been put in place:
- **Shared Drive:** Created folders for financials, media assets, public sheets, and working designs.
- **Curation Tracker:** Created a central sheet to map 100+ maker organizations, sponsors, speakers, and volunteers. Edit access was assigned to prevent data duplicates.
- **Portal Development:** Abhiram is building a simple portal under the GitHub organization to house minutes and track outreach progress.

### 3. Sponsorship & Marketing Strategy
The committee reviewed requirements for the event prospectus and marketing campaign:
- **Sponsorship Tiers:** Agreed on three distinct tiers (Platinum, Gold, Silver) with customized deliverables.
- **Speaker Sponsorship:** Discussed coverage for speaker travel, accommodation, and workshop fees.
- **Marketing Timeline:** Will scale gradually: beginning with ecosystem curation, transitioning to community stories and team updates, and concluding with large public campaigns.

### 4. Experience & Venue Design
The team discussed building an engaging, interactive event flow rather than a simple festival structure.
- **Layout:** Proposed a central open stage bordered by experience stations, hands-on workshop tables, and community booths.
- **Budgeting:** Formulated a cost-conscious budget approach. Prior previous production estimates were INR 4.8 lakhs; the team intends to optimize this year's budget while maintaining quality sound, minimal stage builds, and optimal safety lighting.
