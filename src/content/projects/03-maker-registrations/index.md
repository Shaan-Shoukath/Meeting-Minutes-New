## Milestone Goal
Open public project submissions for individual makers, school/college student groups, and community workshops. The goal is to reach 100+ high-quality applications across hardware, art, and DIY science.

## Action Steps
1. **Forms & Hosting:** Deploy the online registration database page. Ensure applicants can list dimensions, electrical loads, water access, and visual display needs.
2. **Ecosystem Outreach:** Coordinate with college lab networks, FabLabs, and student developer clubs to pitch direct-entry stalls.
3. **Curation Workflow:** Form the curation subcommittee to review applications weekly, filter commercial/resell items, and verify open-source details.
