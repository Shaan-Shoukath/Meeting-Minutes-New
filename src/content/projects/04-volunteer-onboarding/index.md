## Milestone Goal
Onboard 120+ volunteers to manage attendee queues, maker booth technical support, guest check-ins, safety patrols, and social media coverage.

## Action Steps
1. **Application Launch:** Publish the volunteer sign-up form targeting engineering, art, and media students.
2. **Squad Allocation:** Divide applicants into functional squads:
   - *Welcoming Squad:* Ticketing and helpdesk.
   - *Maker Support Squad:* Stall power, water, and technical troubleshooting.
   - *Track Managers:* Workshop room support.
   - *Media Team:* Photography and live social updates.
3. **Training sessions:** Host in-person and online briefings at the venue to map shifts and first-aid checkposts.
