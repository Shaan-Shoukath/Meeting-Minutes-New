## Milestone Goal
Finalize the floor map layout, print all directional and sponsor signage, allocate stalls to selected makers, and verify safety protocols.

## Action Steps
1. **Stall Allocation:** Assign physical booths based on maker utility lists (such as ventilation needs for laser cutters/soldering stations).
2. **Safety Clearances:** Inspect power distribution blocks, coordinate smoke exhausts, and secure municipal/fire department permits.
3. **Print Collaterals:** Lock dimensions and design files with the printing vendor for stage backdrops, directional posters, and badges.
